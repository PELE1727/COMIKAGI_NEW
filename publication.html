PUBLICATION

ANNOUNCEMENT

COMIKAGI regularly publishes official announcements to keep our members, partners, and the wider community informed. These include updates on mining operations, environmental initiatives, safety measures, and cooperative decisions. Transparency is at the heart of our communication strategy.

Announcements also cover new partnerships, government regulations, and community programs. By sharing timely information, we ensure stakeholders remain engaged and aware of developments that affect them directly.

JOBS AND OFFERS

COMIKAGI provides employment opportunities in the mining sector and promotes professional development through knowledge-based, skill-based, and field-based experience for workers and specialists.

We publish job openings, internships, and training programs to attract talent and empower local communities. Our cooperative values fair contracts, equal opportunities, and career growth for all employees.

STUDIES AND RESEARCH

Research and studies are conducted through systematic investigations to test samples of fresh ore and tailings. These studies support evidence-based decision-making by collecting, analyzing, and interpreting geological and technical data.

Our publications include technical reports, environmental impact assessments, and sustainability studies. Sharing research findings helps improve mining practices and promotes responsible resource management.

NEW MINERALS DISCOVERED

The COMIKAGI–Kababara mine concession contains Amblygonite deposits, a lithium-bearing fluorophosphate mineral containing roughly 8–10% lithium oxide. Found in granite pegmatites, it is sometimes used as a high-value source of lithium.

Discoveries like these highlight the importance of ongoing exploration and research. We document new mineral findings to support innovation, attract investment, and contribute to global knowledge in mining science.
