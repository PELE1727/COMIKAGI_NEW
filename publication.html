PUBLICATION

ANNOUNCEMENT

COMIKAGI regularly publishes official announcements to keep our members, partners, and the wider community informed. These include updates on mining operations, environmental initiatives, safety measures, and cooperative decisions. Transparency is at the heart of our communication strategy, ensuring stakeholders remain engaged and aware of developments.

JOBS AND OFFERS

We provide employment opportunities in the mining sector and promote professional development through knowledge-based, skill-based, and field-based experience. COMIKAGI is committed to empowering workers and specialists by offering fair contracts, training programs, and career growth opportunities.

STUDIES AND RESEARCH

Our cooperative conducts systematic investigations to test samples of fresh ore and tailings. These studies support evidence-based decision-making by collecting, analyzing, and interpreting geological and technical data. Research findings are shared with stakeholders to improve mining practices and environmental sustainability.

NEW MINERALS DISCOVERED

The COMIKAGI–Kababara mine concession contains Amblygonite deposits, a lithium-bearing fluorophosphate mineral containing roughly 8–10% lithium oxide. Found in granite pegmatites, it is sometimes used as a high-value source of lithium. Discoveries like these highlight the importance of ongoing exploration and research in our cooperative.
