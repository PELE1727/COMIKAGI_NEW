SERVICES

MINING

COMIKAGI specializes in responsible mining practices. We extract cassiterite and coltan from pegmatite ores hosted in metasedimentary rocks. Our mining operations prioritize worker safety, environmental protection, and community benefits.

We use modern underground techniques and invest in training programs to ensure our workforce operates efficiently and safely. Mining is not only about resource extraction but also about creating sustainable value for future generations.

LABORATORY WORK

Our laboratories conduct scientific testing and analysis of geological samples to determine chemical composition and quality control. Using advanced XRF methods, we provide accurate data that guides mining decisions and ensures compliance with international standards.

Laboratory work also supports research projects, environmental monitoring, and innovation in mineral exploration. Transparency in testing builds trust with stakeholders and partners.

MINERAL PROCESSING

Mineral processing involves separating valuable minerals from raw ore and waste rock. COMIKAGI applies physical and chemical methods to increase grade and concentration, producing high-quality mineral products.

We continuously improve our beneficiation techniques to reduce waste and minimize environmental impact. Tailings management and recycling initiatives are part of our commitment to sustainability.

MARKET

Metal and mineral prices are influenced by supply and demand, production costs, economic conditions, and global commodity markets. COMIKAGI monitors these trends closely to provide fair and competitive offers to clients.

Our publications include market analyses and forecasts, helping stakeholders make informed decisions. We believe in transparency and fairness in trade.

HOSPITALITY

Hospitality reflects COMIKAGI’s commitment to excellence in service, accommodation, and guest care. We provide facilities that welcome partners, researchers, and community members with professionalism and respect.

Our hospitality services strengthen relationships and foster collaboration, ensuring that visitors experience comfort and trust in our cooperative.
