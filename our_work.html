OUR WORK

MINING METHOD

COMIKAGI applies underground mining methods designed to maximize safety and efficiency. Our operations focus on extracting mineral deposits beneath the surface in pegmatite ores hosted in schist and metasedimentary rocks. Techniques such as room-and-pillar mining are selected based on orebody shape, depth, and strength to optimize recovery and cost-effectiveness.

In addition to traditional underground methods, COMIKAGI continuously evaluates modern mining technologies to improve productivity and reduce operational risks. Regular geological mapping, rock stability assessments, and ventilation control systems are implemented to ensure safe working conditions for all personnel.

MINERAL FIELD PROCESSING

Mineral field processing, also known as beneficiation, is carried out directly at the mine site. This process separates valuable minerals from waste rock through sluicing and other techniques. By upgrading the economic value of mined material, we produce high-grade concentrates while managing tailings responsibly to reduce environmental impact.

Our processing systems are designed to minimize waste and maximize resource utilization. Continuous monitoring of processing efficiency allows us to improve recovery rates while maintaining environmentally responsible practices aligned with sustainability goals.

SECURITY

Security is central to our operations. COMIKAGI implements strict measures to protect mined minerals from the field to the office and ultimately to the market. Our protocols ensure traceability, accountability, and compliance with international standards.

Security systems include controlled site access, regular audits, and tracking mechanisms that ensure minerals are handled transparently throughout the supply chain. These measures help prevent unauthorized activities and reinforce trust with stakeholders and regulatory bodies.

COMMUNITY AND SOCIAL ENGAGEMENT

We believe mining should benefit communities. COMIKAGI invests in social, economic, and environmental projects that empower local members to participate in decision-making and increase ownership of initiatives. These efforts boost self-esteem, improve well-being, and build stronger, more resilient communities.

Our community programs include education support, health initiatives, and infrastructure development projects aimed at improving quality of life. We actively collaborate with local leaders and organizations to ensure that our activities align with community needs.

LATEST PROJECTS

In 2026, COMIKAGI launched a community water supply initiative and expanded training programs for young miners. These projects reflect our commitment to sustainability, education, and social responsibility.

Future projects will focus on environmental rehabilitation, renewable energy integration, and continued skills development programs to prepare the next generation of mining professionals.
