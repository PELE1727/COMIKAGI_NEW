COOKIE POLICY

COMIKAGI (“we”, “our”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, provide personalized content, analyze traffic, and ensure compliance with applicable regulations.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow us to remember preferences, recognize users, and enhance site functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website to function properly. Without these, you may not be able to access certain features.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website to improve site performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (like language or region) to provide a personalized experience.
  • Advertising/Targeting Cookies: Track browsing habits to display relevant ads and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality or performance of the website.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (like Google AdSense, analytics providers, and social media integrations) that set cookies on your device. These cookies are subject to the respective third-party privacy policies and may be used to deliver personalized advertising or measure campaign effectiveness.

User Rights

You have the right to withdraw consent, request deletion of stored cookies, and learn more about how your data is processed. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for details on exercising these rights.

Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or using the cookie consent banner provided on the site.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with the “last updated” date. Please check back periodically.
