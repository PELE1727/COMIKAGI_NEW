ABOUT COMIKAGI

WHO WE ARE

COMIKAGI (Cooperative Miniere Kababara et Gikingo) is a legally established mining cooperative based in Gakenke District, Rwanda. With over 35 years of experience in the mining industry, COMIKAGI focuses on responsible mineral extraction, operational excellence, and community development.

OUR HISTORY

Established in 1989, COMIKAGI has consistently operated across multiple licensed mining sites, including the Kababara perimeter. The cooperative has grown into a recognized and trusted entity in Rwanda’s mining sector under the leadership of its president, Kayitesi Beatrice.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to conduct safe, efficient, and environmentally responsible mining while improving the economic well-being of our members and surrounding communities.

WHAT WE DO

We specialize in the extraction and processing of key minerals such as cassiterite and coltan, which are essential in global industries like electronics and manufacturing.

PRODUCTION AND OPERATIONS

Our production capacity ranges between 10,000 kg and 20,000 kg. Cassiterite remains the dominant mineral extracted from pegmatite ores.

TECHNOLOGY AND VALUE ADDITION

We utilize magnetic separator machines to improve mineral quality by removing unwanted materials, ensuring higher purity and market value.

FUTURE PLANS

We aim to introduce smelting processes to refine minerals locally, increase value, create employment, and contribute to Rwanda’s industrial growth.

OUR COMMITMENT

COMIKAGI is committed to ethical practices, environmental protection, transparency, and compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.

TRANSPARENCY & TRUST

This website provides accurate and reliable information about our operations. We aim to build trust with visitors, partners, and stakeholders through transparency and professionalism.
